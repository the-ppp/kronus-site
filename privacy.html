Privacy
Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use this application (the "App").

This App is designed to help you track how you spend your time throughout the day. Your privacy is important, and the App has been built with a minimal data collection approach.

In short:

  • We do not collect your personal data
  • We do not store your data on external servers
  • All your data stays on your device

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information about you.

This includes:

  • No account creation (no email, name, or login details)
  • No tracking of your activity outside the app
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data

Data Stored on Your Device

The App allows you to input:

  • Activities recorded throughout the day (e.g., every 15 minutes)
  • Optional notes associated with those activities

This data is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to us or to any third party.

Data Control and Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • You can delete your data at any time within the App
  • If you uninstall the App, all associated data is permanently deleted from your device

Since we do not collect or store your data externally, we cannot access, modify, or delete it on your behalf.

Third-Party Services

This App does not use any third-party services that collect personal data.

Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security measures (such as passcodes, encryption, and system protections).

While we aim to build a secure application, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

This App is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. However, we do not knowingly collect any personal information from users of any age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact
