How does time tracking work in Kronus?

Kronus lets you log your activities in 15-minute blocks throughout the day. At the end of the day, you'll have a clear picture of how you spent your time.

Is my data stored securely?

Yes. Your data is stored locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Is Kronus available for Android?

Kronus is currently available for iOS. An Android version is in development — stay tuned for updates.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete all your data from within the app at Settings → Data → Delete all data. This action is irreversible.
